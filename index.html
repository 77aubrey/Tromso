Tromso

Fjellheisen

When you come to Tromso, taking the cable car up the mountain to see the panoramic view of the city is a must-arrange itinerary. Feeling the whiteness of Tromso on the top of the mountain, the sunset and pink clouds at sunset, and the deep blue sky exclusive to the polar regions at night is a kind of shock and feeling that cannot be described in words.

Reindeer under the northern lights

At this authentic Sami camp, you can meet herds of reindeer and take part in feeding sessions for these adorable animals. If you are lucky, you can even see the magical Northern Lights, because the camp is located in a perfect place, there is almost no artificial light polluting the air, seeing the Northern Lights in the North Pole while being surrounded by a herd of reindeer is the ultimate Norwegian experience!

Arctic Cathedral

The Arctic Cathedral, with its clean and clear lines complementing the pure white snow, adds a touch of stylish charm to this silvery town. As we walk across the Ruwegen Bridge, the church gets closer and closer, and the tranquility and excitement intertwine to create a free joy. The design-filled church is distinctive, and the warm yellow light inside and the quiet blue outside give a soothing sense of security.
